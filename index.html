I plan to continue my career by producing my first short film soon and try to release it on multiple streaming platforms. I would also like to submit my project(s) to film festivals in order to become globally recognized for my work and in hopes of networking and meeting with individuals in the industry who are intrested in collaborating with me for future film productions.
